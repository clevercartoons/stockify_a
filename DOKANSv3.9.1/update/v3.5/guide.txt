1) How To Update

Note: please check the path properly before replacing any file


- upload errors to root\script\resources\views\
- replace helper.php from root\script\app\Helper\
- replace CronController.php from root\script\app\Http\Controllers\
- replace Frontend/FrontendController.php from root\script\app\Http\Controllers\Frontend\
- replace FrontendController.php from root\script\app\Http\Controllers\
- replace PlanController.php from root\script\app\Http\Controllers\Seller\
- replace ProductController.php from root\script\app\Http\Controllers\Seller\
- replace register.blade.php from root\script\resources\views\marchant\
- replace general.blade.php from root\script\resources\views\seller\settings\
- replace seo.blade.php from root\script\resources\views\seller\store\
- replace app.blade.php from root\script\resources\views\layouts\
