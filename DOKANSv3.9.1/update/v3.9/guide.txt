1) How To Update

Note: please check the path properly before replacing any file



//helper file
- replace helper.php from script/app/Helper/helper.php

//main site
- replace app.blade.php from dokans/script/resources/views/layouts/app.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/layouts

- replace app.blade.php from dokans/script/resources/views/main/app.blade.php  to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/main/

- replace register.blade.php from dokans/script/resources/views/marchant/register.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/marchant/

//for theme
- replace app.blade.php from  dokans/script/resources/views/frontend/arafa-cart/layouts/app.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/frontend/arafa-cart/layouts

- replace app.blade.php from  dokans/script/resources/views/frontend/bazar/layouts/app.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/frontend/bazar/layouts 

- replace app.blade.php from  dokans/script/resources/views/frontend/bigbag/layouts/app.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/frontend/bigbag/layouts

- replace app.blade.php from  dokans/script/resources/views/frontend/saka-cart/layouts/app.blade.php to YOUR_WEBROOT/script/resources/views/frontend/saka-cart/layouts
